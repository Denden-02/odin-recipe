Description

This is an orange stew that's made slightly savory by a mix of seasonings and ingredients including liver spread, soy sauce, garlic, and of course, tomato sauce. It’s a beloved dish among Filipino families because of its warmth and hearty quality!

You can try Panlasang Pinoy's recipe for Pork Kaldereta Sa Gata to taste it for yourself! Feel free to substitute the red meat with chicken as well if you prefer this ingredient in your stew.

Ingredients

  • cutting 3 lbs. of chicken into serving pieces
  • dice 1 potato and 2 carrots.
  • mince 1 onion
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • grate 1 ounce of cheese
  • 8 ounces of tomato sauce
  • 3 ounces of liver spread
  • 4 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 ½ cups of water.

Steps

  1. Marinating our chicken
  2. Frying the potatoes and carrots
  3. Sautéing the onion, garlic, and chicken
  4. Letting the stew boil
  5. Integrating some seasonings and vegetables
