Odin - Oh, Dan! - Recipes

Description bago lutuin

This dish is basically a savory cake that is deep-fried to crispy perfection. Now our grated eggplants and chopped green onions add some more exciting texture to the mix. And of course, to ensure that we get something that's satisfyingly salty, we also season this with some salt and ground black pepper.

This can also be referred to as the vegetable version of Shrimp Ukoy (okoy).

Ingredients bago gawin

  • 5 Chinese eggplants
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 bunch of green onions
  • 4 tablespoons of cornstarch
  • 8 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • ¼ teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon of baking powder
  • 5 teaspoons of salt
  • 2 cups of water
  • 2 cups of cooking oil

Steps para magawa

  1. Seasoning the eggplants
  2. Draining the eggplants of water
  3. Mixing some of the ingredients together
  4. Frying the Crispy Fritters
  5. Making the sauce
  6. Dip the crispy fried eggplant in spicy vinegar dipping sauce
