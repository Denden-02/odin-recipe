Description bago lutuin

This is essentially the classic Filipino favorite but with a special ingredient— plenty of delicious, savory mozzarella cheese! This really upgrades the dish, and it gives it a far more satisfying texture. It also fits perfectly with our ground pork, garlic, bell pepper, and other yummy components.

But other than that stretchy filling, you can also expect a distinctly crunchy outer layer. That''s because we fry the lumpia wrapper to a perfect gliden crisp! Come and check out what you will need to make this irresistibly savory dish!

Ingredients bago gawin

  • 1 cup of minced carrots
  • ¾ cup of breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg some ground black pepper
  • 2 lbs. of ground pork
  • Grab and mince 1 onion
  • 30 lumpia wrappers
  • 2 cups of cooking oil
  • 8 Mozzarella string cheese that are sliced
  • 2 teaspoons of Knorr Liquid Seasoning

Steps para magawa

  1. Making the filling
  2. Assembling the lumpia
  3. Frying the lumpia
  4. Serve Koya well
